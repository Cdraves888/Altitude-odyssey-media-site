FAQ

Common
Questions.

Are you FAA Part 107 certified? +
Currently pursuing Part 107 certification. Commercial drone work (for hire) legally requires this in the US. Web design and digital services are fully available now with no restrictions.
How far do you travel for shoots? +
Within 25 miles at no extra charge. Beyond that, a travel fee of $0.55/mile applies. For larger productions I'm happy to discuss project-specific arrangements.
Do I own the content you create? +
Yes. Once final payment is received you have full ownership of all delivered files. Commercial licensing is included in Tier 02 and above for both aerial and web packages.
What if I need changes after delivery? +
Minor revisions are included in every package. Larger changes or ongoing updates are billed at $60/hr or you can add a monthly maintenance plan at $120/mo.
How long do websites take to build? +
The Landing (single page) typically takes 3–5 days. The Presence (full site) takes 7–14 days. The Platform and custom builds are scoped per project. Rush timelines available for an additional fee.
Do you require a deposit? +
Yes — 50% upfront to begin the project, 50% upon completion before final delivery. This protects both parties and keeps timelines committed.
Can I combine aerial and web services? +
Absolutely — that's what the bundles are designed for. Combining services saves you money and means everything is visually consistent from your footage to your website.
What areas do you currently serve? +
Web and digital services are available nationwide — everything is done remotely. Aerial services are local. Reach out and let's discuss your location and project scope.
