Brand statement

Altitude Odyssey Media is a cinematic aerial brand focused on refined motion, atmosphere, and perspective. Built around a dark, polished visual style, the brand is centered on capturing places, landscapes, and moments from above in a way that feels elevated, immersive, and intentional.

About

Built on atmosphere, motion, and perspective.

Conor Draves Founder of Altitude Odyssey Media

I created Altitude Odyssey Media to build something that feels more cinematic than generic — a brand focused on mood, motion, and perspective from above.

My goal is to create visuals that feel polished, intentional, and memorable while growing a portfolio that reflects a darker, more refined style.

I want the brand to feel premium from the start, with every image, post, and detail matching that standard.
