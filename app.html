FAQ
How long does a website take? +
The Landing (single page) takes 3–5 days. The Presence (full site) takes 7–14 days. The Platform and custom builds are scoped per project.
Do I own everything you build? +
Yes. Once final payment is received you have full ownership of all delivered files, code, and assets.
Is a deposit required? +
Yes — 50% upfront to begin, 50% on completion before final delivery.
Are drone services available now? +
Available with commercial licensing upon Part 107 certification. Web design is fully available now with no restrictions.
