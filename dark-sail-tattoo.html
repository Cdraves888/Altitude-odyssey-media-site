Where Craft Meets Permanence

Dark Sail Tattoo was built on a single belief: every piece of art placed on skin should be intentional, precise, and personal. No flash off the wall. No rushed consultations. Every client leaves with something made for them alone.

With years of experience across blackwork, traditional, and fine-line styles, the work speaks for itself — clean lines, confident shading, designs that age the way good tattoos should.

Carlos Bonfil — Artist & Owner
